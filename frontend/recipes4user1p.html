Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Cuisine: Italian

Dish Type: Main Course

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper
  • Salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti as per packet instructions.
  2. Dice pancetta and fry it in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and mix with grated Pecorino Romano and Parmesan.

Cooking Steps

  1. Drain the spaghetti, reserving some pasta water.
  2. Add the spaghetti to the pancetta and toss over low heat.
  3. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water for creaminess.
  4. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper. Serve immediately.
