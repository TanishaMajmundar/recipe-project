Tomato Basil Soup

Cuisine: Italian

Dish Type: Soup

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 Onion, diced
  • 2 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 2 cups Vegetable Stock
  • 1/2 cup Fresh Basil Leaves
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Cream or Croutons for garnish

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and garlic, and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until softened, about 10 minutes.

Cooking Steps

  1. Add vegetable stock and basil leaves. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Blend the soup using an immersion blender until smooth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with cream or croutons if desired.
