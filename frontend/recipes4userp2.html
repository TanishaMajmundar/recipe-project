Fresh Garden Salad

Cuisine: International

Dish Type: Salad

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: None

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mixed Greens
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 1 Carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 Red Bell Pepper, diced
  • 1/4 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Preparation Steps

  1. Wash and dry all the vegetables thoroughly.
  2. Chop the vegetables as per the ingredient list.
  3. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a small bowl to prepare the dressing.

Serving Instructions

  1. Toss the vegetables in a large salad bowl.
  2. Pour the dressing over the salad and mix gently.
  3. Serve immediately for the freshest flavor.
